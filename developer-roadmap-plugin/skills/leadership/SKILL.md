# Engineering Leadership & Specialization Skill

## Quick Start

Leadership means enabling others to succeed and driving technical direction.

### Key Leadership Competencies
- **Vision Setting**: Clear direction and goals
- **Influencing**: Getting buy-in without authority
- **Communication**: Clear, honest feedback
- **Delegation**: Empowering teams
- **Coaching**: Developing people
- **Decision Making**: Data-driven choices

## Engineering Management

### 1-on-1 Meetings
```markdown
Weekly 30-minute 1-on-1s:

1. **Check-in** (5 min)
   - How are you doing?
   - Anything blocking you?

2. **Individual Topics** (15 min)
   - Their agenda first
   - Career growth
   - Project concerns

3. **Manager Topics** (10 min)
   - Feedback
   - Development areas
   - Company updates
```

### Performance Management
- Clear expectations
- Regular feedback (not just reviews)
- Growth conversations
- Coaching approach

### Team Building
- Psychological safety
- Trust development
- Clear roles and responsibilities
- Shared mission

## Technical Leadership

### Tech Lead Responsibilities
```
Code Quality
↓
Architecture Decisions
↓
Technical Mentoring
↓
Knowledge Sharing
```

### Decision Making Framework
1. **Gather Information**
   - Problem statement
   - Constraints
   - Options

2. **Consult Stakeholders**
   - Engineers
   - Product
   - Users

3. **Decide**
   - Make decision
   - Document reasoning

4. **Communicate**
   - Clear explanation
   - Timeline
   - Next steps

## Technical Communication

### Documentation Best Practices
- **Clear audience**: Who reads this?
- **Purpose**: Why does it exist?
- **Examples**: Show, don't just tell
- **Maintenance**: Keep it current
- **Structure**: Easy to navigate

### Presentation Skills
1. **Structure**
   - Opening: Problem/context
   - Middle: Solution/details
   - Closing: Takeaway/action

2. **Delivery**
   - Speak clearly
   - Maintain eye contact
   - Use visuals effectively
   - Engage audience

3. **Q&A Handling**
   - Listen fully
   - Acknowledge question
   - Answer concisely
   - Offer follow-up

## Mentoring & Coaching

### Coaching Model
```
1. Listen: Understand their situation
2. Ask: Help them think through options
3. Suggest: Offer your perspective
4. Support: Help them execute
5. Celebrate: Recognize growth
```

### Developing Junior Engineers
- Clear onboarding
- Meaningful assignments
- Regular feedback
- Stretch projects
- Pair programming

## Conflict Resolution

### When Disagreement Occurs
1. **Separate people from problem**
   - Respect the person
   - Focus on issue

2. **Understand perspectives**
   - Listen actively
   - Ask clarifying questions

3. **Find common ground**
   - Shared goals
   - Shared values

4. **Collaborate on solution**
   - Brainstorm together
   - Consider options

5. **Agreement & commitment**
   - Clear decision
   - Accountability
